The Fates with Zeus

In the story of Sarpedon and Hector, Zeus is aware that his son Sarpedon will die. He weighs the destinies, learns this outcome, but has no control over it, indicating that even powerful Gods like Zeus could not challenge the Three Fates.

The Fates at the birth of Meleager
